About Us

Enactus, formerly known as Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE), is an international non-profit organisation in 39 countries around the world and has over 70,000 students as active members. Enactus brings together a diverse network of university students, academic professionals and industry leaders around the shared mission of creating a better and a more sustainable world. Enactus Thapar since its inception in 2012, has undertaken several community outreach projects as part of this shared mission.
